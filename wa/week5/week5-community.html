About the Lattice Program

The Lattice Scholars Program at the University of Colorado Boulder supports first-generation, Pell-eligible Colorado students who are pursuing degrees in engineering. The program aims to remove financial barriers by closing the total cost-of-attendance gap, making it possible for students to earn a debt-free engineering degree.

Beyond financial support, Lattice provides mentorship, academic advising, and community-building opportunities that help scholars thrive both in the classroom and in their professional development. The program's goal is to empower first-generation students to build confidence, connections, and long-term success in STEM fields.


Our Mission

Supporting first-generation students with resources, mentorship, and community events.
