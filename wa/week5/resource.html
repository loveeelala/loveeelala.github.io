Scholarship Guide

Applying to the Lattice Scholarship and maintaining it through requirments.

Lattice Scholarships are awarded through the CU Boulder general scholarship application.

  • Eligibility: First-year, first-generation, Pell-eligible Colorado engineering students
  • Submit your application before the posted deadline
For further instructions please visit CU Boulder's scholarship website

In order to keep the Lattice scholarship, scholars must meet the following requirments:

  • Be a part of an engineering student society
  • Maintain a good academic standing
  • Attend monthly 1:1, group, and cohort meetings

Mentorship Program

Learn more about what the Mentorship Program is, and how mentors can help.

Scholars are required to meet 1:1 with mentors and attend a group meeting each month. Meetings are aimed to help scholars become more familiar with the Lattice Program, on-campus resources, and to support the transition from High School to College.

Workshops/Events

Learn more about workshops and events. Check Events page for information on when.

Workshops and events are meant to help foster community within scholars' respective cohorts as well as build on skills they will need during their time at college. The Lattice Program aims to help scholars feel confident with resume-ready building workshops as well as financial literacy and career ready workshops.
